'Manufacturing Control Data' Kaggle Playground Series

exploring clustering algorithms

My first try at unsupervised methods, and observing how different algorithms work/don't work for this dataset. Here, k-Means is too simplistic (creates spherical clusters), dataset density was too uniform and thus density based methods didn't seem to work well either. Gaussian Mixture Models seemed the most accurate thus far.

Also attempted a semi-supervised approach, using 'confident' predictions out of the GMM as labels to train a HistGBM classifier. Doing so managed to eke out a little bit more score-wise. 

PCA reduction wasn't useful either, so only utilised it and t-SNE for visualisation.
